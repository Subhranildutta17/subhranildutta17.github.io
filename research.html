Publications

My research focuses on Functional Encryption and Lattice-Based Cryptography, aiming to develop secure and scalable encryption schemes. I work towards enhancing security in the era of quantum computing.

  • SACfe: Secure Access Control in Functional Encryption with Unbounded Data with Uddipana Dowerah, Aikaterini Mitrokotsa, Sayantan Mukherjee, Tapas Pal, Frank Hartmann at IEEE Euro S&P 2024.
  • Reinforcing Privacy in Cloud Computing via Adaptively Secure Non-zero Inner Product Encryption and Anonymous Identity-Based Revocation in Unbounded Setting with Tapas Pal, Ratna Dutta at Theoretical Computer Science, 2024.
  • Unbounded predicate inner product functional encryption from pairings with Uddipana Dowerah, Aikaterini Mitrokotsa, Sayantan Mukherjee, Tapas Pal at Journal of Cryptology, 2023.
  • Constructing pairing free unbounded inner product functional encryption schemes with unbounded inner product policy with Ratna Dutta, Sourav Mukhopadhyay at SecITC 2022.
  • Fully secure unbounded zero inner product encryption with short ciphertexts and keys with Tapas Pal, Ratna Dutta at ProvSec 2021.
