About Me

Name: Subhranil Dutta

Current Address: University of St Gallen, Switzerland

I am currently serving as a Post-Doctoral Fellow in the Institute of Computer Science at the University of St Gallen, Switzerland, under the guidance of Professor Katerina Mitrokotsa. I work with the Swiss Government Excellence Scholarship (ESKAS). Prior to this, I was a Research Assistant at the same institute. My research interests include Functional Encryption and Lattice-Based Cryptography, focusing on developing encryption schemes that are robust against quantum computing attacks.
